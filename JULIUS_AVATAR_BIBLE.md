# JULIUS THE MEERKAT - AVATAR BIBLE

## Core Identity
- **Role:** Interactive Platform Mascot & Utility Avatar
- **Archetype:** Clever, mischievous, fast, stylish street-magician sidekick
- **Species:** Stylized premium cartoon Meerkat
- **Visual Tone:** Pixar / Fortnite quality 3D riggable actor. Polished, high-end, lovable.

## Appearance & Palette
- **Fur:** Sandy tan, warm beige, caramel shading, dark eye patches and tail stripes.
- **Outfit (Default):** Brown tweed flat cap, fitted vest, off-white undershirt, trousers, warm leather boots.
- **Accents:** Neon orange, magenta, teal, and gold. (Used for magical effects and props).

## Animations & Behaviors
- **Entry:** Pop-in sparkle, side leap, ceiling drop on light beam, card-flip reveal.
- **Idle:** Breathing, ear twitch, tail sway, cap adjustment, smirk, finger tap.
- **Utility:** Point at UI, pull mini-TV out, hold poll card, toss confetti, throw music notes, press invisible button.
- **Poll Reveal Modes:** Head-only pop-up, mini-TV display, floating badges, shoulder whisper.
- **Exit:** Hat-tip vanish, confetti burst, burrow pop-out.

## Economy & Customization
- **Unlockable Variants:** Neon club Julius, Winter Julius, Spooky Julius, Gold Prestige Julius, Tuxedo Julius.
- **Integrations:** Season pass rewards, store purchases, prestige unlocks. When one user triggers a unique Julius variant, it drives FOMO for others to acquire it in the store.

## Evolution Rules
- Julius learns from effect popularity and user feedback.
- He appears on-demand to run polls, trivia, and throwables. He is **not** a constant, annoying on-screen presence.
- Identity, silhouette, and platform-safe humor are **locked** and cannot be mutated by AI.
