# TMI MASTER LAWBOOK

*This document contains the immutable, permanent platform laws governing The Musician's Index & BerntoutGlobal XXL. These laws cannot be altered by AI without direct approval from Marcel or Big Ace.*

## The 15 Permanent Platform Laws

1. **Every issue is monthly.** It must have a governed cover artist rotation and be archived permanently.
2. **Every major event produces an archive.** It must generate a recap article, a replay clip, a reward distribution, and an archive output.
3. **Every show/game must have a complete identity.** This includes a host, a fallback host, a venue, a sound identity, an emote layer, a reward map, a sponsor map, and a safety policy.
4. **Every dynamic feature must have a static fallback.** If a video loop, 3D render, or live widget fails, the system must degrade gracefully to a premium static state.
5. **Reading-first zones stay calm.** The magazine remains readable first, cinematic second.
6. **Motion stays selective, periodic, and premium.** No page-wide restless flickering; max 1-2 moving elements in view at a time.
7. **Sponsor presence must feel native, not annoying.** Brands integrate through prizes, venue surfaces, and story cards.
8. **Premium tiers must never corrupt fairness.** Subscriptions (Bronze to Diamond) enhance access, cosmetics, convenience, analytics, and status—but not competitive outcomes.
9. **Audience participation must matter.** Fans decide the winners through live voting, polls, and prediction engines.
10. **Michael Charlie must see everything.** Every important system, error, and traffic flow must be visible to the Conductor AI.
11. **Seasonal themes may evolve style, but never break structure.** The layout grid is sacred.
12. **Extend first, create second.** Every new feature must attempt to reuse an existing component, module, or page before building a new one.
13. **Every user journey is a closed loop.** Paths must end in discovery, progression, reward, archive, or return.
14. **Every launch must pass verification.** Visual QA, mobile QA, accessibility QA, and fallback checks are mandatory before production.
15. **AI learns within governed boundaries.** Any AI-driven system improvement must be logged, sandboxed, tested, and entirely reversible.
