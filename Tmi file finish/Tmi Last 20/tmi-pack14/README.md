### `brand-protection/BRAND_LANGUAGE_PROTECTION.md`
**The immutable brand language law.**

The permanent platform rule: protected brand words may NEVER be auto-corrected, normalized away, or replaced by standard-English spelling anywhere in the platform, bots, editors, CMS, search, or published content. Every tool must preserve brand spellings first, then correct everything else.

Surface-by-surface rules: VS Code (add to cSpell dictionary, never flag), Grammarly (add to custom dictionary), CMS editor (disable auto-correct on brand terms), search indexing (map variants, never replace), bots (never rewrite brand words), publisher (flag unofficial spellings and suggest correction TO official, not away from it).

Complete table of forbidden replacements: "Burnt Out" for Berntout, "Burnout Global" for BerntoutGlobal, "Berntout Productions" for Berntout Perductions, "Musicians Index" for The Musician's Index, "Cipher Arena" for Cypher Arena, "Stream and Win" for Stream & Win.
