## Platform Advertising — Placement Rules, Types, and Restrictions
### BerntoutGlobal XXL / The Musician's Index

---

## ADVERTISING vs SPONSORSHIP DISTINCTION

These are two separate systems with different rules:

| Dimension | Advertising | Sponsorship |
|---|---|---|
| Relationship | Brand pays for placement | Brand aligns with specific artist/event |
| Scope | Platform-wide placements | Artist/page/event-specific alignment |
| Selection | Automatic (ad network or direct) | Curated by Big Ace or artist |
| Disclosure | "Ad" label required | "Sponsored by [Brand]" label required |
| Crown moment | BLOCKED | BLOCKED |
| Winner reveal | BLOCKED | BLOCKED |
| Tier upgrade | BLOCKED | BLOCKED |

---

## AD PLACEMENT RESTRICTIONS (HARD RULES)

```
NEVER SHOW ADS:
✗ At crown transfer moment
✗ At winner reveal moment
✗ At tier upgrade moment
✗ Over the performer's face
✗ Over the stage center
✗ During active turn in battle mode
✗ Covering the turn timer
✗ Auto-playing audio in rooms
```

---

## AD CONTENT REQUIREMENTS

All ads must:
- Be clearly labeled "Ad" or "Sponsored"
- Meet TMI brand safety standards (no competing music platforms)
- Be approved by Big Ace before going live
- Use pre-approved creative assets stored in Cloudflare R2
- Respect Discovery-First principle: ads don't displace undiscovered artists
