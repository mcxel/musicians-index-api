# AD_PLACEMENT_POLICY.md
## Where Ads Can and Cannot Appear — Complete Policy
### BerntoutGlobal XXL / The Musician's Index

---

## PLACEMENT ALLOWED ZONES

```
✅ Homepage editorial sidebar
✅ Lobby wall footer
✅ Discover page sidebar
✅ Archive page banner
✅ Below article content (not within article body)
✅ Pre-roll before room join (optional, skippable after 5s)
✅ Room side rail (static only, no audio)
```

---

## PLACEMENT BLOCKED ZONES

```
❌ On top of the crown winner card
❌ On top of winner reveal animations
❌ On top of tier upgrade animations
❌ On top of performer face (any room)
❌ On top of stage center (any room)
❌ On top of the turn timer (any room)
❌ Overlapping Diamond tier badge
❌ In the HUD strip bottom bar
❌ Auto-playing in any room audio channel
❌ Within the first 3 seconds of any live event start
❌ During any emergency broadcast
```

---

## AD FORMAT RULES

| Format | Allowed | Max Size | Audio |
|---|---|---|---|
| Static image | ✅ | 600×200px | None |
| Animated GIF | ✅ | 600×200px, max 10s loop | None |
| Video (pre-roll) | ✅ | Max 15 seconds | Optional (muted default) |
| Video (in-room) | 🟡 Side rail only | Max 5 second loop | None |
| Audio-only ad | ❌ | — | Not allowed |
| Interstitial | ❌ | — | Not allowed |
| Pop-up overlay | ❌ | — | Not allowed |
