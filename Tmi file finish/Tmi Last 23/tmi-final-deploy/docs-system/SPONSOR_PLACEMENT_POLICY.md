# SPONSOR_PLACEMENT_POLICY.md
## Where Sponsor Content Can and Cannot Appear — Complete Policy
### BerntoutGlobal XXL / The Musician's Index

---

## SPONSOR PLACEMENT ALLOWED

```
✅ Artist profile sponsor panel (below stats, above articles)
✅ Room side panel (labeled "Sponsored by [Brand]")
✅ Venue hero section (sponsor board)
✅ Event header (co-branded title: "Nike Presents: The Battle")
✅ Magazine article header (branded feature)
✅ Prize card (prize sponsored by brand)
✅ Shared preview slot (sponsor mode — clearly labeled)
✅ Lobby wall sponsor card (position 9 of lobby wall — clearly separated from artist cards)
```

---

## SPONSOR PLACEMENT BLOCKED

```
❌ Crown transfer moment — any point during crown animation
❌ Winner reveal card — any point during winner animation
❌ Tier upgrade animation — from start to finish of full sequence
❌ Artist primary performance area (must not cover performer)
❌ Turn timer display
❌ Diamond tier badge (Marcel/BJ permanent Diamond never obstructed)
❌ Emergency broadcasts
❌ Queue position indicator (audience must always see who is next)
```

---

## SPONSOR DISCLOSURE REQUIREMENTS

Every sponsor placement must clearly show:
- "Sponsored by [Brand Name]" — visible before any interaction
- Brand logo minimum 40×40px
- Sponsor name minimum 12px font
- No deceptive "native content" that looks like editorial

---

## SPONSOR AUDIO RULES

In rooms, sponsor audio is allowed ONLY when:
- User has explicitly clicked/tapped to hear it (no autoplay)
- It plays through the sponsor preview slot (not the room voice channel)
- Voice and sponsor audio can be independently volume-controlled
- Sponsor audio stops immediately when host triggers emergency controls
