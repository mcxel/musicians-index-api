# Bot Activation Rules

This document defines the strict policy for activating any automated outreach bots for The Musician's Index. These rules are in place to prevent premature, uncontrolled, or unwanted automated messaging.

## Core Principle

**Bots must not be activated until the core platform is stable, proven, and ready to receive the traffic they generate.** Automated outreach is a Phase 2+ activity and is not part of the initial launch.

## Activation Pre-flight Checklist

The following conditions **must** be met and verified before any outreach bot system is enabled in the production environment.

| # | Prerequisite | Verification | Status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **Core Platform Live** | The `LAUNCH_PROOF_LADDER` is 100% complete and signed off. | `[ ]` |
| 2 | **Intake Pages Exist** | Dedicated, functional landing pages for Sponsors and Advertisers are live and tested. | `[ ]` |
| 3 | **Intake Forms Work** | The forms on the intake pages correctly capture lead data and route it to a CRM or admin review queue. | `[ ]` |
| 4 | **Analytics Tracking in Place** | Web analytics are configured to track the entire funnel, from bot-driven click to intake form submission. | `[ ]` |
| 5 | **Human Approval Workflow Defined** | The process for a human to review and approve bot-generated messages or target lists is documented and implemented. | `[ ]` |
| 6 | **Compliance and Legal Rules Exist** | Rules for anti-spam, frequency capping, and opt-out handling are implemented and tested. | `[ ]` |
| 7 | **CRM / Lead Queue Ready** | A system is in place to store, view, and manage the leads generated by the bots. | `[ ]` |

## Bot-Specific Activation

Each bot or bot category must have its own activation plan and approval.

### Example: Sponsor Prospect Bot

- **Status:** `INACTIVE`
- **Activation Plan:**
    1. Define target search channels (e.g., LinkedIn, specific industry sites).
    2. Draft and approve message templates.
    3. Run in a "dry-run" mode, logging intended actions without executing them.
    4. Review dry-run logs for accuracy.
    5. Activate with a low daily limit.
    6. Monitor results in the admin review queue.

---
**This policy applies to all bots, including but not limited to:**
- Member Prospect Bot
- Sponsor Prospect Bot
- Advertiser Prospect Bot
- Social Media Content Bot
