## What Changed in This Session

### From Roadmap to Vision

We moved from:
> "Build 7 separate systems sequentially"

To:
> "Use ONE Universal Profile Runtime for all roles, with specialized modules attached"

This single architectural change:
- Eliminates ~60% of duplicate code
- Makes maintenance consistent across all roles
- Accelerates development (shared core = faster role modules)
- Improves reliability (one truth, not six)

To:
> "Show performers deep analytics: conversion rates, audience demographics, booking intelligence, projection models"

This transforms the platform from a content service to a **business tool**.
