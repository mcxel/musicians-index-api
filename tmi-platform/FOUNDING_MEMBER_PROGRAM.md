# Founding Member Program (LOCKED 2026-06-25)

**Authority**: Build Director (Marcel Dickens)  
**Purpose**: Turn early adopters into platform advocates  
**Duration**: Stage 1 → Stage 2 (roughly 2-8 weeks)  
**Benefit**: Revenue flows while platform improves

---

## The Strategy

Early users aren't waiting for a finished product.

They're investing in the future.

Make that investment feel special.

---

## What Founding Members Get

### 1. Founder Badge
- [ ] Lifetime "Founding Member" badge on profile
- [ ] Appears next to username everywhere
- [ ] Purple/gold aesthetic (premium)
- [ ] Tooltip: "Supported TMI from the beginning"

### 2. Early Supporter Recognition
- [ ] Featured in "Hall of Founders" (simple page)
- [ ] Monthly recognition in platform newsletter
- [ ] Priority in community events/opportunities
- [ ] Direct channel to Build Director for feedback

### 3. Lifetime Recognition
- [ ] Founding Member date permanently recorded
- [ ] Anniversary notification (yearly)
- [ ] Special profile designation ("Since [date]")
- [ ] Founding Member tier in Stripe (separate from normal tiers)

### 4. Feature Access
- [ ] Early access to new features (1-2 weeks before general release)
- [ ] Beta testing opportunities
- [ ] Voting on feature priorities
- [ ] Closed feedback channel with product team

### 5. Periodic Updates
- [ ] Weekly "What's New" email during Stages 2-3
- [ ] Direct changelog messages in app
- [ ] Monthly state-of-platform message from Build Director
- [ ] Sneak peeks of upcoming features

### 6. Exclusive Perks (Optional)
- [ ] 10% lifetime discount on any paid tier (if applicable)
- [ ] Free month when launching new feature
- [ ] Exclusive Founding Member community (Discord/group)
- [ ] Direct chat with founders (monthly office hours)

---

## Founding Member Tiers

**Stage 1 Founders** (First 100 members):
- Gold Founder badge
- Full perks above
- Lifetime 10% discount
- Mentioned in launch announcement

**Stage 2 Founders** (Members 101-1000, while in Early Access):
- Silver Founder badge
- Core perks (badge, recognition, early access, updates)
- 5% discount
- Mentioned in release notes

```

As a Founding Member, you get:
- Lifetime Founder Badge
- Early access to new features
- Direct feedback channel with the team
- Lifetime recognition on your profile
- Weekly updates as we improve

We're not claiming to be finished. We're inviting you to build with us.

Join as a Founding Member: [link]
```

### Weekly Updates (Stages 2-3)
- What shipped this week
- What's coming next week
- What Founding Members asked for
- How the platform improved
- A direct message from the Build Director

## Success Metrics (Stages 1-2)

- [ ] 100+ Founding Members in Stage 1 (first 2 weeks)
- [ ] 500+ Founding Members by end of Stage 2
- [ ] 90%+ retention (no cancellations in first 30 days)
- [ ] 80%+ engagement (use features regularly)
- [ ] NPS (Net Promoter Score) >50
- [ ] 50%+ of Founding Members invite friends

---

## Why This Works

1. **Investors feel heard** — They get access to the team and see real improvement
2. **Advocates are created** — Early adopters become the best marketers
3. **Feedback flows directly** — Better than any survey
4. **Revenue flows early** — Cash to fund development
5. **Continuous improvement feels like progress** — Weekly updates keep members excited
6. **Loyalty is built** — Founding Members become lifetime users
