# UNIVERSAL MISSIONS SYSTEM

**LOCKED: April 28, 2026**

**MISSION: Give every user clear daily/weekly/monthly goals that drive retention and engagement.**

---

## I. MISSIONS CONCEPT

Every user gets personalized missions based on their role and level.

Missions are:
- Achievable (no skill requirement, just participation)
- Clear (exact number/goal stated)
- Rewarding (worth doing)
- Varied (stay interesting)
- Progressive (get harder as you level)

## IX. PROGRESSION DIFFICULTY

Missions scale based on player level:

**Tier 1 (Level 1-10):**
- Attend 3 rooms → Attend 20 rooms (daily → weekly)
- Vote 2 times → Vote 10 times

**Tier 2 (Level 11-25):**
- Attend 20 rooms → Attend 50 rooms
- Vote 10 times → Vote 25 times

**Tier 3 (Level 26-50):**
- Attend 50 rooms → Attend 100 rooms
- Support 1 artist → Support 5 artists

**Tier 4 (Level 50+):**
- Attend 100+ rooms
- Maintain streaks
- Reach milestones

## X. ANTI-PADDING RULES

Missions prevent artificial engagement:

```
✗ Micro-attendance farming
  - Must stay 5+ minutes to count
  
✗ Vote spam
  - Cannot vote same contestant twice
  
✗ Tip farming
  - Cannot tip same artist more than once per hour
  
✗ Chat spam
  - Must be actual messages (>3 characters)
  - One message per 30 seconds max
  
✗ AFK completion
  - Must interact during streaming time
```
