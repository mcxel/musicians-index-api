# Big Ace Financial Policy

Big Ace's 10% allocation is a reinvestment reserve for BerntoutGlobal LLC / BerntoutGlobal.com and related approved businesses.
It must remain auditable, logged, capped by policy categories, and visible to Marcel/root.

Operational constraints:
- Every transfer requires a ledger entry.
- No silent withdrawals.
- No user payout/cashout bypass.
- No circular transfer abuse.
- Financial/legal final decisions require Marcel/root approval.

Advisory:
- For the real LLC/books/taxes side, have the accountant or business attorney confirm the exact accounting treatment before any real money moves.
