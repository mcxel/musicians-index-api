Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

Important: This privacy policy applies to The Musician's Index operated by BerntoutGlobal LLC.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email, username, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Name, bio, profile picture (optional)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Stripe (we do not store card numbers)
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent
  • Cookies: See our Cookie Policy

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process payments and subscriptions
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR/DSGVO)

We process your data based on:

  • Contract: To provide services you've signed up for
  • Consent: For marketing communications (you can opt out anytime)
  • Legitimate Interest: For fraud prevention and service improvement
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with laws and regulations

4. Data Sharing

We only share your data with:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Hosting Providers: Hostinger (secure data centers)
  • Email Service: For transactional emails only
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Your Rights (GDPR/DSGVO)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correct: Update inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in machine-readable format
  • Object: Opt out of processing for marketing
  • Restrict: Limit how we use your data

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@berntoutglobal.com

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for:

  • Active accounts: As long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: 30 days (for recovery), then permanently deleted
  • Payment records: 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Logs: 90 days

7. Security

We protect your data using:

  • SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS)
  • Encrypted passwords (bcrypt)
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

8. Cookies

We use cookies for authentication and analytics. See our Cookie Policy for details.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place for EU data (Standard Contractual Clauses).
