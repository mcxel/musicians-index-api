Gifts can be refunded to the original purchaser only. The recipient cannot request a refund.

If you used a promotional code, the refund amount will be the actual amount paid, not the original price.

8.3 Bundled Products

Bundle purchases may only be refunded as a complete package, not individual items.

8.4 Third-Party Services

Purchases made through third parties (App Store, Google Play, etc.) are subject to their refund policies. Contact the platform directly for refunds.
