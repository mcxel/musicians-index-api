## HOST VOICE PROFILES

## JULIUS SOUND IDENTITY

Julius communicates through sound, not words.

- **Chirps**: Curious, light meerkat sounds for passive idle moments
- **Chuckles**: Slightly chipmunk-like but unique — short, playful
- **Alert Sounds**: Sharp chirp on danger/surprise
- **Celebration**: Quick trill + bounce sound
- **Curiosity**: Rising tone, sniff, head tilt sound
- **Mischief**: Low cheeky giggle
- **Split Mode**: Quick pop-burst sound per clone
- **Prop Deploy**: Soft "whoosh" + landing sound per prop type
- **Stretch (meerkat lookout)**: Low soft grunt → slow breath

**Rule**: Julius never talks in full sentences. He communicates in personality sounds.

## VEX SOUND IDENTITY

VEX does NOT speak. Ever.

### Proximity Warning System
| Distance | Sound |
|---|---|
| Far (warning zone) | Faint trumpet tease, marching-beat underpinning |
| Mid (approach zone) | Louder brass hit, percussion kick |
| Close (escort zone) | Full warning sting — trumpet + percussion together |
| Contact/escort | Short payoff sting + costume-specific sound variant |

**Rule**: Volume and intensity scale with proximity — not instant full blast.

## UNIVERSAL AUDIO RULES

- No host should sound like another host
- Sound identity is a brand lock — same as visual identity
- Scripts must vary — no repeated opener within same session
- Crowd-reactive audio is separate from host voice
- All audio must have silent/reduced mode for accessibility
- No audio plays automatically on page load unless user is in a live event
