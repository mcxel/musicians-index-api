# HOST_BLOCKING_AND_DIALOGUE_SYNC.md
## Movement Synced to Speaking

---

## SYNC RULES
- Host may walk during transitions between segments
- Host pauses movement when asking questions
- Host gestures during speech emphasis
- Host faces contestant when engaged in conversation
- Host faces camera for reveals and announcements

## DURING SPEECH
| Moment | Movement |
|---|---|
| Question delivery | Still, facing contestant |
| Emphasis point | Single gesture with off-hand |
| Reveal | Turn slightly toward board/screen |
| Crowd address | Turn toward audience or camera |
| Wrap-up | Step back slightly |

## LISTENING DURING WALK
- Slow walk allowed during contestant response
- Cannot approach or retreat during emotional contestant moment
- Pause completely for winner reveals

# SEATED_HOST_PERFORMANCE_SYSTEM.md
## Monthly Idol Panel Hosts — Full Seated Animation Library

---

## PURPOSE
Monthly Idol hosts must feel alive and expressive while seated.
Seated ≠ frozen.

## ANIMATION LIBRARY

### Idle
- Breathing cycle
- Subtle chair sway
- Eye movement / blink
- Hand rest adjustment
- Foot tap (hidden below table)
- Slight head tilt

### Reaction - Mild
- Lean forward (interested)
- Lean sideways to co-host
- Chin to hand (deliberation)
- Note-taking gesture
- Table tap (emphasis)

### Reaction - Strong
- Quick half-stand (1–2 seconds)
- Strong forward lean
- Both hands on table
- Head shake (disagreement)
- Empathetic lean-in
- Quick turn to face co-host

### Event Moments
- Full stand for winner reveal
- Full stand for surprise/shock
- Return to seat with clear settle animation
- Proud lean-back (satisfaction)
- Applause seated (clapping while sitting)
- Deliberation huddle with co-host

## RETURN TO SEAT RULE
After any stand: host reseats within 4–6 seconds
unless the scene holds them standing.
