# LIVE_VENUE_EVENT_SYSTEM.md
## Audiences Inside Environments — All Live Event Types

---

## CORE ANSWER: YES

Every venue environment works across all live event types.
The same rooms that host shows also host:

- Cyphers
- Battles
- Live Streams
- World Concerts
- Premieres
- Going Live (artist broadcast)

The audience sits INSIDE the environment and watches from their seat.
Artists perform on the stage or appear on the main screen.
Everything is live, reactive, and visible.

---

## THE FUNDAMENTAL RULE

```
ARTIST OR PERFORMER → on stage or on screen
AUDIENCE → seated in venue, watching from their seat
HOST → positioned at their zone
JULIUS → floating crowd support
VEX → standby (if elimination show)
ENVIRONMENT → fully alive (lights, sound, crowd motion)
```

The audience does not watch a flat stream.
They sit in a venue and experience it.

## AUDIENCE PERSPECTIVE SYSTEM

Audience members each have a seat in the venue.
From their seat they see:

```
Front rows:    close to stage, large detail view
Mid rows:      balanced view of stage + crowd
Back rows:     full overview, see entire room
Balcony:       bird's-eye cinematic view
VIP box:       angled private view
```

**Sightline rule**: Every seat in every venue always has an unobstructed view of the stage and main screen.

---

## AUDIENCE INTERACTION IN EVENTS

While seated, audience members can:
- React with emojis/sounds (crowd reaction system)
- Tip the artist
- Vote in live polls (Julius deploys poll board)
- Cheer (adds to crowd energy meter)
- Join the hype meter
- Send messages (moderated)
- Bookmark the moment
- Share clip

---

## ARTIST PERSPECTIVE

Artists performing or going live can SEE their audience:
- Animated crowd visible from stage view
- Crowd energy meter visible
- Viewer count visible
- Live reaction feed visible
- Chat feed visible
- Julius may pop into artist view with tips/alerts

---

## SCREEN vs STAGE PRESENCE

### SCREEN STAGE (Live Stream / Concert / Premiere)
Artist appears on the main screen surface.
Full cinematic quality.
Screen may expand, glow, and react to the performance.

### AVATAR STAGE (Cypher / Battle / Going Live with avatar)
Artist's avatar appears live on center stage.
Full 360 motion.
Stage lights respond in real time.
Crowd sees avatar performing at full scale.

### HYBRID (World Concert / Premium Going Live)
Screen + avatar overlay.
Screen shows real video.
Avatar reactions float over or beside it.
Stage lights coordinate with the stream.
