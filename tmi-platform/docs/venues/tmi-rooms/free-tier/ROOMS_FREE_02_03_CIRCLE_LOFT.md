# FREE TIER — COMMUNITY CIRCLE ROOM
## Room Pack — Complete Specification

---

# ROOM IDENTITY
**The Circle**
Tier: Free (Green) | Code: `ROOM_FREE_02`

## Concept
A circular room where audience wraps around a central floor.
Used for cyphers, fan discussions, listening parties, intimate freestyles.
Inspired by community circle spaces — slightly raw, highly connected.

## Feel
- Community-first
- Everyone equal
- No "back of the room" disadvantage
- Raw, intentional

## Color Identity
- Base: warm concrete, muted terracotta, weathered teal
- Accent: Green glow ring on floor center (tier identity)
- Light: Overhead industrial pendants, slight color in center ring

---

# ROOM LAYOUT
```
╔═══════════════════════════╗
║   AUDIENCE (curved)       ║
║  ╔═══════════════╗        ║
║  ║               ║        ║
║  ║  CIRCLE STAGE ║        ║
║  ║  (center)     ║        ║
║  ╚═══════════════╝        ║
║   AUDIENCE (curved)       ║
╚═══════════════════════════╝
```

Seating wraps 270° around center.
One opening (30°) = entry zone.

Seats: 16–24 max (curved benches / stools)
Stage: Center floor circle (glowing ring, no platform)

---

# ROOM SEATING MAP
- Curved bench seating wraps around stage
- All seats face center
- No row has better view than another
- Bench style: simple wood/metal with tier-color cushions
- Floor: cracked concrete with glowing center ring

---

# ROOM SIGHTLINES
- 360° view of center stage from all seats
- No blocking possible (open center)
- Screen: Small circular overhead display for digital content
- Artist can spin and address all seats

---

# ROOM STAGE AND SCREEN
- Stage: Floor-level glowing circle (no platform)
- Screen: Circular overhead display or side wall panel
- Center glow: Green ring that pulses with energy and crowd reactions
- No curtain — open circle format

---

# ROOM LIGHTING AND CURTAINS
- Industrial pendant lights hanging from ceiling
- Center ring glow (reactive)
- Events:
  - Stream start → center ring lights up
  - Energy peak → ring pulses outward in waves
  - Tier upgrade → ring changes color (green → bronze wave)
- No curtains (open format)

---

# ROOM AUDIO PROFILE
- Acoustic: Concrete-room reverb, slightly live
- Feel: Underground community space
- Crowd: Intimate chatter
- Center effects are spatially centered

---

# ROOM HOST BLOCKING
- No dedicated host zone
- Julius may appear standing in the ring
- Julius is at ring-size scale
- Cyphers: Host stands in ring, artists rotate in/out

---

# ROOM CROWD BEHAVIOR
Cap: 24 viewers
- Idle: subtle swaying, head bobs
- Performance: leaning in from all sides
- Peak: some stand from bench seats, clapping in rhythm
- Tier upgrade: crowd looks around, lights up

---

# ROOM CAMERA MAP
- RING VIEW: camera orbits slowly around center
- PERFORMER: from center looking outward at crowd
- OVERHEAD: bird's eye of full circle
- CLOSE: tight on performer in ring

---

# ROOM ANIMATION RULES
Always animating:
- Center ring glow breathes
- Crowd subtle sway
- Pendants sway slightly

Event triggers:
- Beat drop → ring pulses outward
- Cheer → ring flashes
- Upgrade → color transformation from center out

---

# ROOM EVOLUTION PATH
Circle → Bronze Meeting Hall or Bronze Bar Room

Transformation: Ring glow shifts from green to bronze → walls expand → ceiling rises → seating shifts to proper chairs → floor upgrades

# FREE TIER — RAW BRICK LOFT
## Room Pack — Complete Specification

---

# ROOM IDENTITY
**The Loft**
Tier: Free (Green) | Code: `ROOM_FREE_03`

## Concept
A raw brick loft space.
Exposed pipes, aged concrete, original wooden beams.
Open floor plan with a small elevated stage area.
The kind of place where underground legends get started.

## Feel
- Gritty
- Authentic
- Underground-prestige
- "This is where it begins"

## Color Identity
- Base: dark brick red, aged concrete gray, rust amber
- Accent: Green neon trim on pipes and ceiling beams (tier identity)
- Light: Edison bulbs, exposed rigs, industrial pendant lights

---

# ROOM LAYOUT
```
╔════════════════════════════╗
║  BRICK BACK WALL           ║
║  STAGE PLATFORM (raised 1) ║
╠════════════════════════════╣
║  FLOOR ZONE (open)         ║
║  MIX OF STANDING + SEATED  ║
║  ROW 1: 6 folding chairs   ║
║  ROW 2: 5 stools/standing  ║
║  BACK: Standing zone (10)  ║
╠════════════════════════════╣
║  ENTRY (industrial door)   ║
╚════════════════════════════╝
```

Seats: 21–30 total (mixed standing/seated)
Stage: Small raised platform (1 step up), brick-backed

---

# ROOM SEATING MAP
- Front rows: folding chairs (branded)
- Second area: bar stools and standing
- Back: standing general area
- All face stage naturally (angled sight lines)
- Floor: polished concrete with green edge detail

---

# ROOM SIGHTLINES
- Stage is raised 1 level — everyone sees over seated row
- Standing area: full view of stage
- Back wall has a monitor for overflow view
- No serious blocking issues

---

# ROOM STAGE AND SCREEN
- Stage: Raised brick platform, 1 step, small
- Back wall: Exposed brick with projected screen
- Screen tech: projector-style display on brick wall
- Side: Small monitor on industrial mount

---

# ROOM LIGHTING AND CURTAINS
- Edison bulbs on exposed wire grid
- 1-2 stage spotlights (moveable)
- Green pipe trim glow
- Events:
  - Stream start → spotlights activate, edisons dim
  - Energy peak → bulbs flash in rhythm
  - Tier upgrade → all lights shift tone, walls begin to expand
- No curtains — raw space

---

# ROOM AUDIO PROFILE
- Acoustic: Brick reverb, medium-tight
- Feel: Underground club, warm but edgy
- Crowd: Energetic murmur, closer volume
- Bass: Present and natural in this space

---

# ROOM HOST BLOCKING
- Host (rotation) can stand on stage platform
- Julius appears near stage edge or pipes
- Crowd: general open floor
- No seated host zone (this is a standing room)

---

# ROOM CROWD BEHAVIOR
Cap: 30 viewers
- Idle: Standing crowd sways, some talking
- Performance: Crowd presses forward
- Peak: Front crowd jumps slightly, back crowd raises arms
- Tier upgrade: Collective reaction — lights change, crowd cheers

# ROOM CAMERA MAP

- OVERHEAD: above the exposed beam grid

- CLOSE: tight on performer face

- Dust particles in light beams
- Crowd idle sway

- Beat → bulbs pulse in rhythm
- Cheer → sparks on pipe trim
- Upgrade → beam color shifts, walls start expanding animation
