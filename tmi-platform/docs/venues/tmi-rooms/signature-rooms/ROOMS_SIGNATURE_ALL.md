# SIGNATURE ROOM 1 — THE CONCERT HALL
Code: `ROOM_SIG_01`

The flagship premium concert hall. The gold standard.
Architecture, history, warmth. Where monthly idol and major events live.

- "You made it"

- Base: deep rich mahogany, velvet red, warm gold
- Accent: Gold railing, chandelier, stage trim

## Seating Classes
- Stalls/Orchestra: Premium
- Dress Circle: VIP
- Balcony: General (still excellent view)
- Box seats: Elite (side angles)

## Lighting
- Central chandelier (animated, reacts to events)
- Full theatrical rig (12+ positions)
- Stage proscenium arch lighting
- Sconce wall lighting (ambient)
- Events: chandelier brightens on winners, dims for dramatic moments

## Audio Profile
- Concert hall acoustic: rich, warm, deep
- Full stereo image
- Natural reverb of large hall
- Crowd: proper concert audience (hushed for performances, erupts at peaks)

## Curtain System
- Full velvet curtain (gold trim)
- Open: dramatic slow reveal OR fast hype open
- Close: slow ceremonial, OR blackout cut
- Intermission curtain: semi-open pause mode

## Host Blocking
- Stage has host positions left/right
- Judge panel zone (front stage area)
- Monthly Idol panel fully supported
- Julius: stage edge, balcony mode possible

## Camera Map
- CONCERT_EPIC: full hall from top balcony
- STAGE_HERO: from front rows up at stage
- BALCONY_LOOK: from balcony looking down at orchestra
- ARTIST_POV: from stage seeing 340 people
- CHANDELIER: from chandelier position
- CLOSE_STAGE: tight on performance

## Evolution Path
SIGNATURE — does not transform to another room.
Evolves internally:
V1 → V3: better lighting tech, better crowd density, more reactive chandelier, improved screen tech
